About me

Hi! My name is Raphaël, I'm passionate about birds, data and sharing! I designed these webapps to make birding more fun and make it easy to visualize and share bird related data!

Zoziologie goes back to my childhood, when my brother made fun of my passion for birds (“oiseaux” in French) by calling it “zozio”. As I grew older and pursued scientific studies, I tried to combine my passion with birds (zozio) with my scientific background (logos), that gave birth to a new discpline: zoziologie!
